01. nina & harminder

The Thomas Fogarty Winery in Woodside was the perfect location for this beautiful Indian Hindu Wedding Ceremony. Nestled in the Santa Cruz mountains and surrounded by sprawling vineyards with stunning views, the picturesque venue provided a romantic backdrop for the couple to exchange their vows. The bride meticulously handpicked every detail and color of the wedding and reception, resulting in a truly breathtaking event.

02. manpreet & aman

The wedding that captured the hearts and eyes of many has won several International Photography Awards, including the prestigious wedding album award from WPPI. This week-long event was a stunning Indian Sikh destination wedding at the luxurious Grand Velas Resort in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.

03. tamim & ayesha

The Wedding Extravaganza was nothing short of a "Big Fat Indian Fusion Wedding" that left everyone awestruck. This grand event was possibly the biggest Indian/Fusion wedding ever hosted in the Bay Area, California. Three massive halls of the Santa Clara Convention Center were transformed into breathtaking event sites, with one each dedicated to the Sangeet, Wedding Ceremony, and Reception.

04. shiv & mona

Shiv and Mona's Indian Arabic Fusion wedding was a beautiful celebration of love and cultural diversity. This wedding consisted of multiple events, each with its unique charm and beauty. The Arabic wedding was held at the ultra-beautiful Ritz Carlton Half Moon Bay, a property that never fails to amaze us with its stunning views.
